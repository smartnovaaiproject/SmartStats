SmartStats — Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 9, 2026

SmartStats ("the app") is published by SMARTNOVA SOLUTIONS LLC. This policy explains what data the app handles. The short version: your data never leaves your device.

Data collection

SmartStats does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. The app has no user accounts, no analytics, no advertising SDKs, and no tracking of any kind.

Data storage

All information you enter (projects, stats, quotas, daily entries, optional project photos) is stored locally on your device only, using the device's local storage. It is never uploaded to any server. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Backups

The app can export a backup file at your request. That file is saved where you choose (e.g., your device storage or your own cloud drive). You control it entirely; we never receive a copy.

Permissions

The app may request access to your photo gallery only when you choose to set a project photo. The selected image is stored locally inside the app's data and is not transmitted anywhere.

In-app review

The app may occasionally invite you to rate it using Google Play's standard in-app review dialog. This dialog is provided and operated by Google Play; the app does not receive or store any information from it.

Children

The app does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children, because it collects no data at all.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this address with a new effective date.
