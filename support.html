Common questions

Where is my data stored?
Entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server.

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?
Yes. Deleting the app removes all locally stored data.

How do I set a quota?
Open a project and tap "Set Quota" at the bottom of the screen.
